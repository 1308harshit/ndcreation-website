# Complete Website Redesign - Gaming/Sci-Fi Tech Theme

## Vision
Transform NDcreations into a dominant, powerful tech brand with neon purple/pink gaming aesthetics combined with futuristic sci-fi elements. The site should feel like a high-end gaming/tech company that builds the future.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Neon Purple (#B026FF, #8B5CF6)
- **Secondary**: Hot Pink/Magenta (#FF006E, #EC4899)
- **Accent**: Electric Cyan (#00F0FF)
- **Background**: Deep Black (#0A0A0F, #1A1A2E)
- **Highlights**: Neon Green (#39FF14) for success states

## Design Style
- Gaming-inspired bold typography
- Sci-fi holographic effects
- Glitch animations
- Particle systems with purple/pink glow
- Hexagonal patterns
- Neon glow effects everywhere
- Cyberpunk grid overlays
- Dominant, powerful visual hierarchy

## Logo Requirements
- Must look professional yet dominant
- Incorporate neon purple/pink
- Modern, tech-forward design
- Works well at all sizes
- Memorable and unique
- Could include: geometric shapes, circuit patterns, or abstract tech symbol

## Key Features to Redesign
1. **Hero Section**: Massive 3D scene with purple/pink neon particles, holographic elements
2. **Navigation**: Futuristic with glow effects
3. **Cards**: Hexagonal or angular shapes with neon borders
4. **Buttons**: Glowing, pulsing effects
5. **Typography**: Bold, gaming-style fonts
6. **Animations**: Fast, snappy, powerful
7. **3D Elements**: More prominent, interactive
8. **Background**: Animated grid, particles, nebula effects

## Pages to Redesign
- Homepage (complete overhaul)
- Products page
- Services page
- Contact page
- About page

## Technical Requirements
- Maintain Next.js 15 structure
- Keep existing functionality (forms, admin, etc.)
- Improve 3D performance
- Add more particle effects
- Implement glitch animations
- Add holographic shaders
