🎯 WHAT THE AI CHATBOT WILL DO

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. 📧 AUTO-REPLY TO EMAILS
   • Monitors contact form submissions
   • Reads customer inquiries
   • Generates intelligent responses using Claude AI
   • Sends professional replies automatically
   • Logs all conversations

2. 💬 AUTO-REPLY TO WHATSAPP
   • Monitors WhatsApp messages
   • Reads customer questions
   • Generates conversational responses using Claude AI
   • Sends replies automatically via WhatsApp Business API
   • Logs all conversations

3. 🧠 INTELLIGENT AI (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)
   • Understands customer needs
   • Knows about your services, pricing, capabilities
   • Answers common questions professionally
   • Escalates complex questions to human
   • Maintains conversation context

4. 📊 ADMIN DASHBOARD
   • View all AI conversations
   • Take over conversations manually
   • Mark conversations for review
   • Monitor AI performance
   • Provide feedback to improve responses
